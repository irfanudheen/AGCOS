FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some common questions about AGCOS services, membership, and financial benefits. We aim to provide farmers with transparent and hassle-free support.

Who can become a member of AGCOS?

Any individual engaged in farming or agricultural activities can apply for membership in AGCOS and access our financial and support services.

What types of financial services do you offer?

We provide fixed contributions, recurring Contributionss, Contributions accounts, agricultural loans, agricultural gold loans, and recurring Contributions cum loan facilities tailored for farmers.

How quickly can I get a loan approved?

Agricultural Loan approvals are fast and hassle-free, provided you meet the eligibility requirements and submit the necessary documents. Many of our Agricultural loans are processed within a few days.

Is my money safe with AGCOS?

Yes. AGCOS operates under cooperative society principles, ensuring transparency, accountability, and security of member funds.

How do recurring Contributions cum loan schemes work?

Members Contributions a fixed amount monthly into their RD account, and based on the Contributions value, they become eligible for loans at attractive interest rates.
